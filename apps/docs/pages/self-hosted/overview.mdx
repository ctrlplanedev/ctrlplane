# Overview

Ctrlplane is still in its early days, not everything is perfect yet, and hiccups
may happen. Please let us know of any suggestions, ideas, or bugs that you
encounter on our Discord.

Ctrlplane can be self-hosted, giving you the flexibility to run it on your own
infrastructure.
